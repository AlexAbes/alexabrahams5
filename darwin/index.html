The Darwinian Revolution

This project aims to visualize the links between the historical scientists and academics in the intellectual journey towards the publication of Origin of Species in 1859.

Click and drag the nodes to find out more about that person and their links to others.

The pro-evolutionists are in blue, while the anti-evolutionsist are in pink, and those who did not take a side are in maroon.


By Alex Abrahams for the 2016 offering of CULTBLF 47 at Harvard College.
